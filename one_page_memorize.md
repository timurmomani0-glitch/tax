# One-page memorisation sheet (exam execution)

> Cite the exam's printed tax table for rates. Numbers below are 2024/25 UK
> defaults — verify on exam day.

## IT order
Total → Net → PA (taper £1/£2 >£100k; £0 at £125,140) → Taxable → NSI → SI → DI
→ − Reducers → − PAYE → Payable.
- PSA: £1k BR / £500 HR / £0 AR
- DA: £500
- SR savings: £5,000 (only if NSI < £17,570)
- NSI rates: 20 / 40 / 45 | SI: 20 / 40 / 45 | DI: 8.75 / 33.75 / 39.35

## Employment
- Car: (LP − cap contrib ≤£5,000) × CO₂% × months/12 − PU payments
- Fuel: fixed × CO₂% (all-or-nothing)
- Loan: balance × ORI − interest paid (exempt ≤ £10,000 throughout year)
- Exempt: mobile (1), trivial ≤£50, staff function ≤£150/head, workplace parking, eye tests, pension employer contrib

## Trading add-backs (P-C-C-F-E-L)
Private, Capital, Customer entertaining, Fines, Excess-CO₂ lease (>50 g/km → 15% disallow), Legals on capital.
Also: depreciation, general provisions, gifts unless ≤£50 + ad + not food/drink/vouchers.
Deduct: pre-trade (7y), strip non-trading income, CA.

## Capital allowances
- AIA £1m → allocate to SPECIAL pool FIRST
- FYA 100% on new zero-emission cars / EV charge points; Full expensing: companies only
- WDA: Main 18% | Special 6% (time-apportion short/long AP)
- Cars: 0 g/km = FYA (new) | ≤50 = Main | >50 = Special | any PU (sole trader) → single-asset
- Disposal = lower of proceeds and original cost
- Balancing allowance: only on cessation (pool) or disposal (single-asset)
- Private-use restriction: apply to the allowance, not the pool

## CGT
- Per-asset gain → CY losses (mandatory) → b/f losses (to AEA only) → AEA → rates
- BR band remaining = £37,700 − taxable income
- Residential 18 / 24; Other 10 / 20; BADR 10% up to £1m lifetime; AEA £3,000
- Chattels: P & C ≤ £6k exempt; P > £6k & C ≤ £6k → gain = min(actual, 5/3 × (P − 6k))
- PPR: final 9 months always; deemed occupation rules
- Shares matching: same-day / next-30-days / s104 pool
- Residential: 60-day report & pay

## Corporation tax
- TTP = Trading + Property + NTLR + Gains − QCDs
- Augmented = TTP + dividends from non-51% group companies
- Limits: £50,000 / £250,000 ÷ (n+1) × AP/12
- Small 19% | Main 25% | MR = 3/200 × (U − A) × (TTP / A)
- Pay: 9 months + 1 day; CT600: 12 months after AP end

## VAT
- Rates: SR 20 / RR 5 / ZR 0 (input YES) / Exempt (input NO)
- Register: rolling 12-month > £90k OR next-30-day alone > £90k; dereg £88k
- Blocked: client entertaining; car with any private use; non-business; director accommodation
- Partial exemption de minimis: exempt input ≤ £625/month AND ≤ 50% of total → recover all
- Bad debt: >6 months from due date & written off
- Errors: correct on next return if ≤ greater(£10k, 1% × turnover); cap £50k
- File & pay: quarter end + 1 month + 7 days (MTD)

## NIC
- Cl1 EE: (earnings − PT) × main rate to UEL; excess × 2%
- Cl1 ER: (earnings − ST) × secondary rate; no upper cap; Employment Allowance £5k (eligibility check)
- Cl1A on taxable BIKs
- Cl2 flat (verify year)
- Cl4: (profits − LPL) × main; excess above UPL × 2%

## Admin
- SA paper 31 Oct | online 31 Jan
- POA 31 Jan + 31 Jul (due if prior IT+Cl4 > £1k and >20% uncollected)
- Late filing: £100 / £10 per day (max 90) / 5% (or £300) / 5% (or £300)
- Late payment: 5% at 30 days / 5% at 6 months / 5% at 12 months + interest
- Enquiry window: 12 months from filing
- CT600: 12 months; CT pay: 9 months + 1 day

to the MLRO under MLR 2017.
